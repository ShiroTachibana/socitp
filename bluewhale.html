Blue Whale

The first confirmed sighting of blue whale in the Philippines was in 1758. Recently there have been 33 sightings of blue whales in the Philippines between 2004 and 2019 and 13 of those have been confirmed as a blue whale. The blue whales are considered as the largest mammals existed reaching a maximum confirmed length of 29.9 meters (98 ft) and weighing up to 199 tonnes
