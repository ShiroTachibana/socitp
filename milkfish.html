Milkfish or Bangus

The milkfish is referred as the National Fish of the Philippines. The milkfish aquaculture first occured in the
Philippines around 1800 years ago and spread to Indonesia, Taiwan and the Pacific.
In 1970's, the farmers successfully spawned breeding fish. However, they were hard to obtain and produce
